Punjab is a northern state in India and Pakistan known for its agricultural prosperity.
The name Punjab means “land of five rivers”. The five rivers of Punjab are the Beas, Chenab, Jhelum, Ravi, and Sutlej. 
It is the site of the holiest Sikh shrine, Sri Harmandir Sahib (Golden Temple), in Amritsar.
Talking about Punjabi culture, we must mention its famous festivals like Baisakhi.
Punjab has a rich history of brave warriors and freedom fighters.
It is also famous for its mouth-watering cuisine: Makki di Roti and Sarson da Saag.
The state plays a crucial role in India’s agricultural output.
Punjabi music is famous not just in India but worldwide.
